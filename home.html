ABOUT US


Change Seekers is a non-government organization (NGO) which helps poor people to earn their livelihood in different ways. Our major objective is welfare work in relation to poverty and disease or in respect to social groups such as the elderly or children.

GOALS

Education

Education is the primary source for children to keep their life in safe way. Our NGO provides education material to poor children upto elementary section.

Health

Poverty is a major cause of ill health and a barrier to accessing health care when needed. Our NGO tries to provide better health facilities to poor people.

Human Rights

Poverty is a human rights violation. Every woman, man, youth and child has the human right to a standard of living adequate for health and well-being, to food, clothing, housing, medical care and social services.
